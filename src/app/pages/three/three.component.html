This project is very large and ambitious since it is the first time that such a system is used in the company. Its function expedited the way in which wholesale sales processes were carried out.
Its main functions are as follows.
- Register an order at the customer's establishment.
- Maintain customer sales and credit history.
- Schedule visits to customers automatically, so that the seller at the beginning of the day has his customer list.
- Keep track of the sales that each seller makes to keep his commission acurate.
